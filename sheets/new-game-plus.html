Remembrance Duplication

  • The Ruined Labyrinth and Mausoleum Compound Mausoleums cannot duplicate the remembrances of Shardbearers.

Remembrances

  • Remembrance items can be obtained only once per playthrough, so duplicate a remembrance only if you want both of its items. There are more Remembrances (25) than duplication coffins (10), so choose wisely.

Bell Bearings

  • Offer them to the Twin Maiden Husks before starting NG+ to keep the items unlocked forever.

NPC Bell Bearings

  • These can be obtained from completing their respective questlines. They do not carry over, so stock up on their items before starting NG+.

Scrolls and Prayerbooks

  • Give them to Miriel and buy the unlocked spells before starting NG+.
